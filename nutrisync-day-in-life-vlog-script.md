## üé¨ Production Notes

### Overall Vibe
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimal, intentional
- **Music:** Calm lo-fi/ambient (no lyrics) - consistent throughout
- **Pacing:** Steady, reflective, productive
- **Color Grading:** Slightly warm, natural, not overly saturated
- **Audio:** Room tone + ambient sounds under music (typing, gym sounds, etc.)

### Text Overlay Style
```
Time Headers: SF Pro Display Heavy, 60pt, White, centered
Activity Labels: SF Pro Display Medium, 36pt, White, centered
Subtitle Text: SF Pro Display Regular, 32pt, White, left-aligned, bottom third
    - Conversational, casual tone
    - Max 2 lines per subtitle
    - Natural thought process
```

### NutriSync Integration Strategy
**SUBTLE BUT CLEAR** - App appears naturally as part of productivity routine:
- Phone screens visible but not forced
- Quick glances, natural interactions
- Lime green UI (#C0FF73) stands out but doesn't dominate
- Show impact on energy/focus through subtitles
- Position as tool that enables the productive day, not the star

## üé® Post-Production Specifications

### Color Grading
```
Base LUT: Natural, slightly warm
Highlights: +5
Shadows: +10 (keep details visible)
Saturation: +3 (subtle)
Temperature: +200K (warm, inviting)

Special grades:
- Morning scenes: Cooler, blue tint (+100K offset)
- Library: Neutral, clean
- Gym: Slight desaturation (-5), contrast +10
- Evening: Warmest (+300K)
```

### Text Overlays - Exact Specs

**Time Headers:**
```css
Font: SF Pro Display Heavy
Size: 60pt
Color: White (#FFFFFF)
Stroke: None (or subtle 2px black at 20% opacity)
Position: Center screen, upper third
Animation: Fade in 0.3s, hold, fade out 0.3s
Background: None (or subtle dark vignette)
```

**Activity Labels:**
```css
Font: SF Pro Display Medium
Size: 36pt
Color: White (#FFFFFF)
Position: Center screen, below time
Animation: Fade in 0.2s (0.1s after time), hold, fade out
```

**Subtitle Text:**
```css
Font: SF Pro Display Regular (NOT Bold)
Size: 32pt
Color: White (#FFFFFF)
Stroke: 4px Black (#000000) at 60% opacity
Position: Lower third, left-aligned with 40px margin
Max Width: 80% of frame width
Max Lines: 2
Line Height: 1.3
Animation: Fade in 0.15s, hold, fade out 0.15s
Background: None (rely on stroke for readability)
Timing: Sync precisely with shot, 1-3 seconds per subtitle
```

**NutriSync App UI (when visible):**
- Ensure lime green (#C0FF73) is color-accurate
- No color grading on phone screens (keep UI pure)
- Use mask/selective color to preserve app colors

### Audio Mix

**Layers:**
```
Layer 1: Music (lo-fi/ambient, no lyrics)
  - Volume: -22dB average
  - EQ: Slight high-pass at 80Hz
  - Compression: Light (2:1)
  - Consistency: Same track throughout entire video

Layer 2: Ambient sounds (room tone, typing, gym sounds)
  - Volume: -28dB to -32dB (subtle under music)
  - Purpose: Add texture, realism
  - EQ: Cut below 100Hz to avoid muddiness

Layer 3: Key sound effects (very minimal)
  - Phone notification: -20dB (clear but not jarring)
  - App UI sounds: -24dB (if using any)
  - Shutter click: -22dB
  - Gym weights clinking: -26dB
```

**Music Selection Criteria:**
- Lo-fi hip hop, ambient, or minimal piano
- 70-90 BPM (calm, steady)
- No dramatic builds or drops
- Consistent energy throughout
- Royalty-free: Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or similar

**Recommended tracks to search for:**
- "Calm morning study beats"
- "Minimal ambient focus music"
- "Lo-fi without vocals"
- Similar to: SwuM, Philanthrope, Psalm Trees

### Pacing & Editing Notes

**Timing Strategy:**
```
Act 1 (Morning): 1:15 (25% of video)
Act 2 (School/Library): 1:15 (25% of video)
Act 3 (Workout/Robotics): 1:10 (23% of video)
Act 4 (Evening): 1:05 (22% of video)
Closing: 0:15 (5% of video)
```

**Shot Length Guide:**
```
Most shots: 3-5 seconds
Longer holds: 6-8 seconds (for important moments or text)
Quick cuts: 1-2 seconds (montages, transitions)
App interactions: 5-8 seconds (let viewer read/understand)
```

**Transition Style:**
- 95% straight cuts (clean, modern)
- 5% subtle cross dissolves (0.2s, only for time jumps)
- NO fancy transitions (swipes, zooms, etc.)

**Subtitle Timing:**
- Appear 0.2s after shot starts
- Stay for 2-5 seconds (depending on length)
- Disappear 0.3s before shot ends
- Never overlap between shots
